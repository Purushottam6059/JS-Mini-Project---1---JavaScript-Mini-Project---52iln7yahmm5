ENT
The hospital boasts of highly skilled team of doctors who use latest technologies to perform endoscopic nasal surgeries, skull base surgeries, ear and cochlear implant. Treatment of ear, nose and throat conditions in adults and children, along with problems in hearing and deafness, ringing in the ears, cancers of the ENT are treated. The hospital makes sure to use the latest technologies in this field and keeps updating their team for the same.
